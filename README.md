# GenAI PRD Templates

A collection of practical PRD templates for building AI-native products — including copilots, RAG systems, agentic workflows, and AI assistants.

These templates are designed to bridge the gap between:
- product thinking
- AI capabilities
- real-world system constraints

---

## Why this repo exists

Traditional PRDs are not enough for AI products.

AI PRDs need to handle:
- probabilistic outputs
- evaluation frameworks
- trust and guardrails
- human-in-the-loop decisions
- cost and latency constraints

This repo provides structured templates for that.
